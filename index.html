The Method

One sentence stating your point beats a phrase headline every time.

Michael Alley developed the Assertion-Evidence method at Penn State. I contributed to his research as a student and co-author. I've kept teaching and applying what he found ever since.

Assertion

The headline is a full sentence stating the one claim worth remembering.

Evidence

The body shows proof: a chart, image, or diagram, instead of restating the claim in text.
